1. I forgot my password. How do I obtain it?

Click on Forgot password, enter your registered mobile number, and an SMS with a password reset link will be sent to you. If you experience difficulty, you may also contact ASQ Support.

3. What can I enter visitors/tenant when I login?

4. How to change language in CVIRMS software

After logging in, click on the change language option given above on the website.

5. How should I check out Guest?

You can check out a guest by opening the Visitor List tab, selecting the guest you want to logout, and clicking on the Upload check-out option. Then, select the checkout option and fill in the checkout date and time.

7. After submitting entry notification, a pop-out says "Mentioned data already"

Go to Chrome settings to remove saved data that is repeating and enter new data correctly.
